SECTION 4: OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY

4.1.  The Receiving Party, Kvell Dynamics, shall maintain the Confidential Information in strict confidence and shall not disclose the same to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party, Kishore.

4.2.  The Receiving Party shall restrict access to the Confidential Information to those employees, contractors, or agents who have a legitimate need to know and who have been informed of and agree to be bound by the terms of this Agreement.

4.3.  The Receiving Party shall take all necessary and reasonable measures to protect the Confidential Information from unauthorized disclosure, including but not limited to, using a secure network and password-protected systems, maintaining up-to-date virus protection, and limiting access to authorized personnel.

4.4.  The Receiving Party shall not use the Confidential Information for any purpose other than as necessary to perform its obligations under this Agreement, and shall not reproduce, distribute, or otherwise disseminate the Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.

4.5.  The Receiving Party shall ensure that all employees, contractors, and agents who have access to the Confidential Information are aware of and agree to be bound by the terms of this Agreement, and shall require them to execute a non-disclosure agreement that is substantially similar to this Agreement.

4.6.  The Receiving Party shall store the Confidential Information in a secure and organized manner, and shall maintain accurate and up-to-date records of all Confidential Information received, including but not limited to, the date and time of receipt, the source of the information, and the identity of the person or entity providing the information.

4.7.  The Receiving Party shall notify the Disclosing Party immediately upon discovery of any unauthorized disclosure or breach of the Confidential Information, and shall cooperate fully with the Disclosing Party in any investigation or action taken to remedy the breach.

4.8.  The Receiving Party shall not remove or export any Confidential Information from the jurisdiction of India without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.

4.9.  The Receiving Party shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations in connection with the receipt and handling of the Confidential Information, including but not limited to, data protection and privacy laws.

4.10. The Receiving Party's obligations under this Section 4 shall survive the termination of this Agreement and shall continue in perpetuity.
