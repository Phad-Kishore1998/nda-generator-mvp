SECTION 4: OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY

4.1.  The Receiving Party shall keep the Confidential Information of kishore confidential and shall not disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of kishore.

4.2.  The Receiving Party shall use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing its duties and obligations under the employment relationship with kishore, and shall not use it for any other purpose, including but not limited to, personal gain or benefit.

4.3.  The Receiving Party shall take all necessary and reasonable measures to protect the Confidential Information from unauthorized disclosure or access, including but not limited to, ensuring that all employees, contractors, and agents who have access to the Confidential Information are aware of and agree to maintain its confidentiality.

4.4.  The Receiving Party shall ensure that all employees, contractors, and agents who have access to the Confidential Information are bound by obligations of confidentiality and non-disclosure that are at least as restrictive as those set out in this Section 4.

4.5.  The Receiving Party shall store the Confidential Information in a secure and confidential manner, and shall ensure that all physical and electronic storage facilities are secure and protected from unauthorized access.

4.6.  The Receiving Party shall immediately notify kishore in writing if it becomes aware of any unauthorized disclosure or access to the Confidential Information, or if it suspects that the Confidential Information may have been compromised in any way.

4.7.  The Receiving Party shall cooperate fully with kishore in any investigation or inquiry into any suspected unauthorized disclosure or access to the Confidential Information.

4.8.  The Receiving Party shall not copy, reproduce, or transmit the Confidential Information except as necessary to perform its duties and obligations under the employment relationship with kishore.

4.9.  The Receiving Party shall not remove or export the Confidential Information from India without the prior written consent of kishore.

4.10. The Receiving Party shall not use any trade secrets or proprietary information of kishore for the benefit of any third party, including but not limited to, any competitor of kishore.
