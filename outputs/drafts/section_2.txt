SECTION 2: DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

2.1 For the purposes of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" means all information, whether written or oral, that is disclosed by Kishore to Kvell Dynamics, including but not limited to:

2.1.1 Technical Information, including source code, algorithms, architecture, and other technical data and documentation;
2.1.2 Financial Information, including projections, budgets, revenue data, and other financial information;
2.1.3 Business Information, including strategies, plans, market research, and other business-related information;
2.1.4 Customer Information, including names, contacts, purchase history, and other customer-related data;
2.1.5 Legal Information, including contracts, intellectual property filings, compliance documents, and other legal-related information.

2.2 Confidential Information shall not include information that:

2.2.1 Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Kvell Dynamics;
2.2.2 Was known to Kvell Dynamics prior to its disclosure by Kishore;
2.2.3 Is independently developed by Kvell Dynamics without use of or reference to the Confidential Information;
2.2.4 Is disclosed to Kvell Dynamics with the express written consent of Kishore or as required by law or by a court of competent jurisdiction.

2.3 Confidential Information shall be designated as such by Kishore at the time of disclosure, or by a clear and conspicuous legend or marking on the Confidential Information. In the absence of such designation, Confidential Information shall be deemed to include all information disclosed by Kishore to Kvell Dynamics.
