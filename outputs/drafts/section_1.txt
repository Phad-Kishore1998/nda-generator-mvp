SECTION 1: PURPOSE, PARTIES, AND CONTEXT

1.1. This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on April 09, 2026 ("Effective Date"), by and between Kishore ("Disclosing Party") and Kvell Dynamics ("Receiving Party").

1.2. The Disclosing Party is an employee of Kvell Dynamics, selected for a job position, and will be sharing confidential information with the Receiving Party in the course of their employment.

1.3. The purpose of this Agreement is to establish the terms and conditions under which the Disclosing Party will share confidential information with the Receiving Party, and to ensure that such information is kept confidential and not disclosed to any third party.

1.4. The confidential information to be shared includes, but is not limited to, technical information related to source code, algorithms, and architecture, financial information related to projections, budgets, and revenue data, business strategies and plans, market research, customer data, and legal information related to contracts, IP filings, and compliance documents.

1.5. The Receiving Party acknowledges that the confidential information will be shared with them in order to facilitate their employment with Kvell Dynamics and to enable them to perform their job duties effectively.

1.6. The parties agree that this Agreement is intended to be a reasonable and necessary measure to protect the confidential information of Kvell Dynamics, and that it is a material term of the employment relationship between the parties.

1.7. By executing this Agreement, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
