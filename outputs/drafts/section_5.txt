SECTION 5: PERMITTED DISCLOSURES AND EXCEPTIONS

5.1. Disclosure is permitted when Kvell Dynamics is legally compelled to disclose confidential information by a court of competent jurisdiction or other government authority, provided that Kvell Dynamics gives Kishore prompt written notice of such compelled disclosure and cooperates with Kishore to limit the scope of such disclosure.

5.2. Disclosure is permitted when the confidential information was in Kvell Dynamics' possession prior to its receipt from Kishore, or was already known to Kvell Dynamics at the time of receipt, as evidenced by written records.

5.3. Disclosure is permitted when the confidential information has entered the public domain through no fault of Kvell Dynamics.

5.4. Disclosure is permitted when Kvell Dynamics independently develops similar confidential information without using or relying on the confidential information received from Kishore.

5.5. Disclosure to advisors and affiliates of Kvell Dynamics is permitted, provided that such advisors and affiliates are under a confidentiality obligation at least as restrictive as this Agreement.

5.6. Disclosure is permitted when Kvell Dynamics is required to disclose confidential information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or regulatory requirements, provided that Kvell Dynamics gives Kishore prompt written notice of such disclosure and cooperates with Kishore to limit the scope of such disclosure.

5.7. Disclosure is permitted when Kvell Dynamics is required to disclose confidential information to its auditors or accountants, provided that such auditors or accountants are under a confidentiality obligation at least as restrictive as this Agreement.

5.8. Disclosure is permitted when Kvell Dynamics is required to disclose confidential information in connection with a merger, acquisition, or other change of control of Kvell Dynamics, provided that the acquiring party is under a confidentiality obligation at least as restrictive as this Agreement.

5.9. Disclosure is permitted when Kvell Dynamics is required to disclose confidential information to its lawyers, provided that such lawyers are under a confidentiality obligation at least as restrictive as this Agreement.

5.10. Disclosure is permitted when Kvell Dynamics is required to disclose confidential information to its insurance carriers or brokers, provided that such insurance carriers or brokers are under a confidentiality obligation at least as restrictive as this Agreement.
